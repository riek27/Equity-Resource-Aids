About ERA

A national movement for equity, dignity, and lasting change

Equity Resource Aid (ERA) is a non-profit national NGO dedicated to creating an equitable society by investing in education, peace-building, livelihoods, food security, nutrition, and economic empowerment.

Working hand-in-hand with communities, local authorities, and international partners, we design context-driven interventions that restore dignity, strengthen resilience, and open pathways of opportunity for the most vulnerable across South Sudan.

Our Mission

To empower vulnerable communities through sustainable solutions that promote dignity, resilience, and opportunity.

Our Vision

A peaceful and equitable society where every individual has access to opportunities and resources.
