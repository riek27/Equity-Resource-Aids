How to Apply

Our Recruitment Process

We are committed to a fair, transparent, and merit-based recruitment process.

1. Submit Your Application

Complete the online application or send your CV and cover letter.

2. Application Review

Our recruitment team reviews all applications against the job requirements.

3. Interview & Assessment

Shortlisted candidates may be invited for interviews and practical assessments.

4. Selection

The successful candidate receives an employment offer and onboarding information.

Volunteer With Us

Share Your Time & Skills

Volunteering with ERA‑SS is a rewarding experience. Whether you're a student, professional, or retiree, your time and expertise can change lives in South Sudan.

We have opportunities in teaching, healthcare, data entry, communications, community mobilization, and event support. Volunteers receive orientation, mentorship, and a certificate of service.

Why Volunteer?

  • Make a tangible impact in vulnerable communities
  • Gain hands‑on field experience in development work
  • Build your network with local and international professionals

ERA-SS is committed to creating a diverse, inclusive, and respectful workplace. We welcome applications from qualified candidates regardless of gender, age, disability, ethnicity, religion, or background.
